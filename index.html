FAQ.

Straight answers to your questions.

What services do you offer?
We offer landing page design, full website design, website revamps, responsive optimisation, website audits, and domain & hosting setup — everything you need to get online and grow.
How long does a website take to build?
Most projects go live within 1–2 weeks. The timeline depends on the scope and how quickly feedback is shared. We'll give you a clear estimate before we start.
How much does a website cost?
Every project is priced based on its scope and requirements — there's no one-size-fits-all. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
Will my website work on mobile?
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across all screen sizes — mobile, tablet, and desktop. Mobile-first design is a standard part of our process, not an add-on.
Do you help with domain and hosting?
Yes. We handle the complete setup — from choosing and registering your domain to configuring hosting and getting everything live. We handle all the technical heavy lifting so you can focus on your business.
What happens after the website launches?
We don't disappear after delivery. We offer post-launch support to fix any issues, make updates, and ensure everything keeps running smoothly. You'll always have someone to reach out to.
